# E-Commerce Analytics - Business Report

## Executive Summary

Analyzed 100K+ orders from a Brazilian e-commerce platform (2016-2018)
to identify revenue drivers, customer behavior, and operational improvements.

---

## Key Insights & Recommendations

### 1. Revenue is Growing But Slowing

- **Finding:** Revenue grew 12x from 2016 to 2018, but MoM growth
  decelerated in late 2018
- **Recommendation:** Diversify into underperforming states and launch
  re-engagement campaigns for churned customers

### 2. Repeat Customer Rate is Critically Low (~3%)

- **Finding:** 97% of customers make only 1 purchase
- **Recommendation:**
  - Launch a post-purchase email series
  - Offer first-repeat-purchase discount (10%)
  - Implement a loyalty points program
- **Estimated Impact:** Even a 5% improvement → R$ 675K additional revenue

### 3. Delivery Time Directly Impacts Satisfaction

- **Finding:** Late deliveries receive avg 2.5⭐ vs 4.3⭐ for on-time
- **Recommendation:**
  - Under-promise delivery dates by 2-3 days
  - Partner with regional carriers in northern states
  - Send proactive delay notifications
- **Estimated Impact:** 10% reduction in late deliveries → 0.2 point
  review score improvement

### 4. Top 5 Categories = 45% Revenue

- **Finding:** bed_bath_table, health_beauty, sports_leisure,
  furniture_decor, computers_accessories dominate
- **Recommendation:**
  - Increase inventory depth in top categories
  - Cross-sell accessories within these categories
  - Run targeted promotions during low-sales months

### 5. Credit Card Dominates (74%)

- **Finding:** Credit card is preferred, with avg 3.5 installments
- **Recommendation:**
  - Offer 0% installment plans for orders > R$200
  - Partner with banks for co-branded promotions
  - Add digital wallet options (PIX) for faster checkout

### 6. Peak Shopping: 10AM - 10PM

- **Finding:** 70%+ orders between 10AM-10PM, peak at 2-4PM
- **Recommendation:** Schedule marketing campaigns and flash sales
  during 10AM-2PM to capture early-day shoppers
